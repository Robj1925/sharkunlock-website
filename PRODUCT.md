# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Drivers in North Jersey (Passaic, Paterson, Monmouth Junction, Toms River, Little Falls, Jersey City) who require court-mandated ignition interlock devices (IIDs) to regain their driving privileges. The demographic is highly concentrated and approximately 50% Spanish-speaking. They are often stressed, anxious about compliance, and looking for fast, reliable, local service without hidden fees.

## Product Purpose
A bilingual marketing and lead-capture website that acts as the primary digital storefront for Shark Unlock Interlock. It must out-compete national chains (like Intoxalock) in local SEO, provide frictionless onboarding/intake, and offer clear device training resources to reduce support calls.

## Brand Personality
Professional, authoritative, reassuring, local, and accessible. 
Three words: Reliable, Compliant, Frictionless.

## Anti-references
- Clunky, slow-loading corporate portals with hidden fees.
- Generic SaaS templates that don't speak to the local New Jersey driver.
- The previous Zoho Sites iteration which suffered from high-friction forms and poor SEO visibility.

## Design Principles
- **Frictionless Bilingualism**: English and Spanish must be first-class citizens, switchable instantly without losing context.
- **Local Dominance**: Physical service center locations must be prominent and easily indexable by Google Maps.
- **Anxiety Reduction**: Clear steps, upfront answers, and accessible training videos to calm stressed customers.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for legibility (especially for older drivers or those in varying lighting conditions).
- WCAG AA compliance.
- Full bilingual (English/Spanish) support across all critical paths.
